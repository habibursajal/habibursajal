## 👨‍💻 About Me
I am a researcher and developer dedicated to **Computer Vision**, **Deep Learning**, and **Explainable AI (XAI)**. My focus is on creating interpretable AI solutions for agricultural and biological challenges, specifically within the context of Bangladesh.

## 🏆 Featured Projects

### 🥭 Mango Leaf Variety & Disease Classification
A specialized pipeline utilizing **DenseNet121** and **Explainable AI** to identify mango leaf varieties and diseases. This project focuses on high transparency, using heatmaps to justify model decisions.

### 🐜 Hybrid Ensemble Insect Classification
A high-accuracy classification system for agricultural pests, leveraging a hybrid ensemble of **EfficientNetB0**, **MobileNetV2**, **Swin-Tiny**, and **DeiT-Tiny**.

### 📊 Explainable AI (XAI) Research
Implementation and analysis of Grad-CAM, LIME, and other interpretability techniques to bridge the gap between complex neural networks and human-understandable insights.
